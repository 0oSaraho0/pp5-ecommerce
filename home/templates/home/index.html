About Laneys Loft

Laney loft is a family run business. We were born out of lockdown. Like everyone else we had alot of spare time on our hands and decided to have a good old clear out. All of the charity shops were closed so we decided to set up our own on3 online and raise money for local charitys.

Since then we have grown extensively and we now take donations of preloved clothes snd childrens toys from people all over the country.

Please take some time to look through our site, buy some second had items or complete the donations form and arrange a collection of your own stuff.
